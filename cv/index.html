Summary I am an academic and a researcher with interest in NLP. I have a PhD and nearly ten years of work experience. I am a co-founder of HausaNLP and Arewa Data Science. My research specializes in natural language processing in low-resource settings, focusing on addressing data insufficiency in various NLP applications and solving complex problems with limited data. I aim to bring innovative, data-driven solutions to NLP challenges in a corporate environment.
